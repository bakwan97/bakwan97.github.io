SERVER RULES

Please read carefully and follow these rules to avoid penalties

Punishment Catalog

Warn

Verbal warning (via chat or DM)

Mute

Player cannot chat

Ban

Temporary or permanent server ban

Important Notes

Punishments may escalate faster for severe or repeated offenses

Ban evasion = Immediate Permanent Ban

1️⃣ No Griefing

Breaking, burning, or altering someone else's build without permission.

  • 1st Offense: 12h Ban
  • 2nd Offense: 7d Ban
  • 3rd Offense: Permanent Ban

2️⃣ No Stealing

Taking items from other players' chests, farms, or storage.

  • 1st Offense: Warn + Item Return
  • 2nd Offense: 3d Ban
  • 3rd Offense: 7d–Permanent Ban

3️⃣ No Hacking or Cheating

No X-ray, fly hacks, kill aura, auto-clickers, or unfair mods.

  • 1st Offense: 12h Ban
  • 2nd Offense: 7d Ban
  • 3rd Offense: Permanent Ban

4️⃣ Respect All Players

No harassment, bullying, hate speech, or targeted abuse.

  • 1st Offense: Warn or 1h Mute
  • 2nd Offense: 1d Ban
  • 3rd Offense: Permanent Ban

5️⃣ No Spam or Advertising

No message spam, server ads, or links to other communities.

  • 1st Offense: Warn or 30m Mute
  • 2nd Offense: 1d Ban
  • 3rd Offense: Permanent Ban

6️⃣ Keep Language/Usernames Appropriate

No offensive names, NSFW content, or excessive profanity.

  • 1st Offense: Warn or 1h Mute
  • 2nd Offense: 1d Mute or Kick
  • 3rd Offense: 3d Ban

7️⃣ No Bug Exploits / Duping

Abusing glitches or duplication bugs is not allowed.

  • 1st Offense: 3d Ban + Rollback
  • 2nd Offense: 7d Ban
  • 3rd Offense: Permanent Ban

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about our rules

You can report a player by:

  1. Using the command /report [username] [reason]
  2. Taking a screenshot/video as evidence and uploading it in the #ticket channel on our Discord
  3. Tagging online staff for important reports

For minor first-time offenses, a warning is usually given. If it was truly unintentional and you are cooperative, staff will help explain the rules in more detail. However, for major offenses like hacking, there is no tolerance even if it was unintentional.

The duration of punishments varies depending on the severity of the offense:

  • Mute: 1 hour to 7 days
  • Temporary Ban: 1 day to 6 month
  • Permanent Ban: For severe offenses such as hacking, severe griefing, or extreme toxic behavior

All rule updates will be announced through:

  • In the #peraturan channel on the Discord server
  • This website page which will always be updated
It is recommended to check the rules at least once a week.
