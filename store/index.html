  • Bank transfers (BCA, Mandiri)

  • QRIS (Quick Response Code Indonesian Standard)
  • Convenience store payments (Alfamart, Indomaret)

For QRIS payments, simply scan the QR code provided during checkout using your mobile banking or e-wallet app. All transactions are processed securely through our payment gateway partners.

Rank activation is typically processed within 1-3 hours after payment confirmation. During peak times, it may take up to 6 hours. You'll receive a notification in-game and via WhatsApp (if provided) once your rank is activated.

Your purchased ranks and permissions will remain intact through server updates. In the rare event of a complete server reset, we will provide special restoration packages for all premium rank holders to compensate for any lost benefits.

All sales are final as digital goods cannot be returned. However, if you experience any issues with your purchase that we cannot resolve, we may offer store credit or alternative solutions at our discretion. Please contact our support team for assistance.
